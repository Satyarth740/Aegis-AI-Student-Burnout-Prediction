# Aegis — AI System for Predicting Student Burnout & Dropout Risk

Aegis is an AI-powered predictive system developed during a 24-hour hackathon to identify students at risk of burnout or potential academic dropout.

The goal is to enable early intervention by educational institutions.

## Problem Statement

Student burnout and dropout rates are increasing worldwide due to academic pressure, mental health challenges, and lack of early detection mechanisms.

Institutions often identify these problems **too late**.

## Solution

Aegis uses machine learning models trained on behavioral and academic indicators to predict:

- Burnout risk
- Dropout probability
- Academic stress levels

This allows institutions to intervene early and support students.

## Features

- AI-based burnout prediction
- Dropout risk analysis
- Student performance indicators
- Early warning system for educators
- Data-driven insights
